What is Breaking Chains

Breaking Chains is a Recovery/Support Group-Based Disc Golf Club - Focussing on ADDICTION RECOVERY, PTSD, ANXIETY, DEPRESSION AND/OR any Comparable Condition.

Breaking Chains was founded by Jay Pry. His goal is to help anyone struggling with "PTSD, depression, anxiety, anything that's going to keep you from living a healthy life." He started this group aftering discovering how much it helped with his stress and anxiety. It began as a small support group but in December of 2023 Breaking Chains became a nonprofit.

Our Goals

  1. Supporting Recovery Through Physical Activity
  2. Building a Supportive Community
  3. Education and Awareness
  4. Reducing Stigma and Promoting Inclusivity
